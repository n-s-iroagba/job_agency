the app is a job agency application similar to indeed, admin CRUD Bank account( for recieving  payment from applicant for job processing payments; the bank accounts are of two types i.e open beneficiary for  amounts less than $4999 and normal for $5000 above), CRUD Joblisting Category/ Industry, CRUD JobListing, CRUD JobListing benefit, CRUD JobListingBenefits (e.g salary per annum, paidTimeOff), CRUD JobListingConditions, CRUD CryptoWallet(for receiving payment in crypto currency), Check server and database health, Mail Applicant, CRUD ApplicationStage(some require payments, with option for push notification and mail for applicant), Mark ApplicationStagePayment as paid or unpaid( with option of push notification and mail for applicant), View new applications completed from the applicants end, view application drafts, view unpaid ApplicationStagePayment, view unverifed  ApplicationStagePayment. For the applicant the job flow is if loggedIn index routes to dashboard, if not it routes to home, on the home screen there is a header to introduce the agency and a job list, clicking apply now on the the job card navigates to login/ sign up page where the user signs up with google or by email and password or logs in, then continues to the job application page, on the dashboard there can see pending stages current unpaid payments and the job list they can also make multiple applications, Applicant can CRUD CV, applicant can upload payment screenshot for payment verification, admin has option to send mail and or push notification to applicant during appropriate cru operations
